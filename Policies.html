Terms and Conditions


For Tenants:


  • Tenants must provide valid identification and payment information.
  • Tenants are responsible for maintaining the property during their stay.
  • No smoking or pets are allowed unless specified in the listing.
  • Tenants must adhere to check-in and check-out times as outlined in the agreement.
  • Any damages must be reported to the owner immediately.

For Owners:


  • Owners must ensure the property is safe and well-maintained.
  • All listings must accurately reflect the property and its amenities.
  • Owners are responsible for handling booking inquiries and communications.
  • Owners must comply with local rental regulations and laws.
  • Any cancellation policies must be clearly stated in the listing.

Cancellation and Refund Policy:


  • Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing at least 48 hours before check-in.
  • Refunds will be processed within 14 days of cancellation approval.
  • A full refund will be issued if cancellation is made more than 14 days before check-in.
  • 50% of the total booking amount will be refunded if cancellation is made between 7 to 14 days before check-in.
  • No refunds will be issued for cancellations made less than 7 days before check-in.
  • Refunds may take longer during peak seasons due to high volume.
  • In the case of a double booking, a full refund will be issued to the affected tenant.
  • Refunds for damages will be assessed based on the rental agreement.
  • This policy does not affect your statutory rights.
  • We reserve the right to modify this cancellation policy at any time.

Rental Agreement:


The rental agreement is a legally binding document between tenants and property owners, outlining the responsibilities and rights of both parties. By confirming a booking, tenants agree to adhere to the terms specified, including payment obligations, property usage guidelines, and procedures regarding check-in and check-out. Property owners, in turn, commit to providing the property in good condition and addressing any issues that may arise during the rental period. This agreement is essential for ensuring a smooth rental experience and fostering trust between tenants and owners.
